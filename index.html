New Chaos Engineering Meetup

Oliver Leaver-Smith

There is a new chaos engineering meetup in town, or rather in the north of England. @chaoticNorth are going to be meeting monthly from January 2020 in cities around the north of England, including Sheffield, Leeds, and Manchester. If you want to be involved with the project then get in contact with ols@chaoticnorth.com.

You can keep up to date with all the goings on by subscribing to the mailing list.
